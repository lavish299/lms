DC Machines

4 credit course

Overview

  • Lectures 42
  • Quizzes 3
  • Duration 32 hours
  • Skill level Beginner
  • Language English
  • Assessments Yes
Course Description

At the end of this course the student should be able to have knowledge of constructional features, principle of operation of various types of transformers and DC machines, analyze magnetic circuits and evaluate the performance of transformers and DC machines.

Learning Outcomes
  • Analyze and evaluate magnetic circuits.
  • Students will be able to understand, analyze and evaluate various transformer configurations.
  • Students will be able to understand, analyze and evaluate various types of Dcmachines.

Curriculum
